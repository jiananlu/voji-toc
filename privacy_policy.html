Introduction

Welcome to Voji ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (Voji).

Information We Collect

Audio Recordings

  • What we collect: Our app uses the device's microphone to record audio for voice-to-text transcription.
  • How we use it: Audio recordings are processed locally on your device to convert speech to text for note-taking and idea capture. Audio recordings are not stored permanently and are only used for the immediate transcription purpose.

User Content

  • What we collect: Ideas, notes, and other content you create within the app.
  • How we use it: To provide you with the core functionality of the app - storing and organizing your ideas and notes.

Authentication Information

  • What we collect: If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information from your Google account.
  • How we use it: To authenticate you and provide personalized services.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore.
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information.
  • Your data is synchronized across your devices when you are signed in.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions)
  • Google Sign-In

Each of these services has their own privacy policies that govern how they handle your data.
